Elizabeth Hernandez

I am a 20 year old Filipino + Puerto Rican Graphic Design student at Eastern Washington University. Digital Art is my main medium.

About my Artwork

There are many things that inspire my art style, but some of the biggest are movies and video games. The world-building that is done in just visuals amazes me, and I strive to test a new way to tell a story in my work every day.
